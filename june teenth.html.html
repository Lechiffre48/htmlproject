Juneteenth

Juneteenth's a holiday that commemerates the end of slavery within the US.

A couple facts about Juneteenth:

  • The name Juneteenth comes from the combination of the words June and nineteent, the date when federal troops arrived in Galveston, Texas in 1865 to free the enslaved people[^1^][1].
  • Juneteenth is also known as Jubilee Day, Freedom Day, or Emancipation Day[^2^][2].
